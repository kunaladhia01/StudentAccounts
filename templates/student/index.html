Maintain your personal transactions

Ever forget how much money you owe? Get lost in a pile of IOU’s? Well, Student Accounts has just the thing. Student Accounts maintains an online bookkeeping service that can be easily accessed with a click of a button. Now, you can easily view past transactions and review what still isn’t paid. With this feature, Student Accounts provides an efficient alternative to the current inefficient system that Academy students have to go through.

Buy Lunches!

Academy Lunches are sure to change with Student Accounts. Organizers have a tiresome experience while managing lunches (such as calculating expenses and taking orders for these lunches). We at Student Accounts felt that this just isn’t efficient at all. We’ve developed this software so lunches (excluding weekly pizza sales) can be preordered ahead of time. Organizers can calculate how much food needs to be brought and profit/expenses with ease. For a school that values efficiency, we simply cannot continue the system we have today.

But... how does this all work?

Simply deposit a sum of money into your account (give this money to one of your class council members, who will manage it). From there, you are able to purchase tickets to lunches and events online. The real-life transactions will be done by the council members themselves, so you don’t have to worry about a thing. For payment, you don’t have to do a thing! Simply remember your pin. Payment doesn't need to be given on the spot, but all IOUs must be paid off by the end of the month.
